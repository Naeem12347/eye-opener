What KINGS COLLEGE BUDO is doing

What KINGS COLLEGE BUDO is doing? We are committed to:

  • Teaching digital citizenship and online safety in our curriculum
  • Providing a safe space to report bullying or online abuse
  • Encouraging positive, respectful behaviour online and offline
  • Working with families to protect students’ wellbeing
